History

AquaVoyage was founded in 1999 with the mission to provide unforgettable white water rafting experiences. Over the years, we have expanded our adventures to include various rivers and locations, ensuring that every trip is a unique and thrilling experience. Our commitment to safety, professionalism, and environmental responsibility has been at the forefront of our operations since day one.

As pioneers in the industry, AquaVoyage has continually pushed boundaries, offering innovative rafting experiences while maintaining the highest standards of quality and customer satisfaction. Our team of experienced guides and staff are passionate about sharing their love for adventure and nature with our guests, ensuring that each journey with AquaVoyage is not only exhilarating but also educational and inspiring.

In the early 2000s, we expanded our services to include family-friendly rafting trips, corporate team-building adventures, and customized excursions for special occasions. By 2010, AquaVoyage had established itself as a leader in eco-friendly tourism, implementing sustainable practices to protect the beautiful waterways we explore. Today, we continue to innovate and grow, offering new destinations and unique experiences that keep our clients coming back year after year.
